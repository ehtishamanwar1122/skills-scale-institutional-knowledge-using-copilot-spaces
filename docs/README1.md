# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

## Overview
This documentation provides an overview of the project management practices used by OctoAcme. These processes ensure consistent planning, execution, and delivery of projects across teams.

## Project Management Processes
OctoAcme follows a structured yet flexible project management approach that includes:

- **Planning and Requirements Gathering**  
  Defining project scope, goals, timelines, and deliverables before execution.

- **Agile Execution**  
  Work is organized into iterative cycles, enabling continuous feedback and incremental improvements.

- **Collaboration and Communication**  
  Teams collaborate through shared documentation and regular updates to ensure alignment.

- **Quality Assurance**  
  Deliverables are reviewed and validated to meet defined standards and requirements.

- **Continuous Improvement**  
  Retrospectives and feedback loops are used to refine processes over time.
